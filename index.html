unPrison Project

The unPrison Project staff speak at prisons across the U.S teaching life skills, cultivating critical thinking, creating post-prison life plans, and helping incarcerated women and girls stay connected with their families. This full stack web application is an administrative portal that was custom built for the unPrison Project nonprofit by a team of four, including myself. It allows unPrison Project staff to record, reference, and edit/add venues and events quickly from anywhere in the world with internet access. It was built with React, Redux, Sagas, Javascript, Node.js, Express, and PostreSQL. See the codehere.Try it here.

Hiking Journal

The Hiking Journal App lets users add, delete, and edit deciduous and coniferous trees to a list of trees identified as they are hiking and exploring. It is a place to keep track of trees you have identified. This app is in continuous development and features are changing rapidly. It is being built with Ionic, Typescript, Firebase, Cloud Firestore, Angularfire, and AngularJS. See the codehere.Try it here.
